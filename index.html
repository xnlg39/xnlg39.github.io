Individuals Impact on Climate Change

What does this website aim to acheive?

Climate change is a phrase that everyone is familiar with, it is a topic that is getting more and more media attention. And yet, whilst most people have a basic idea of what climate change is and are aware of some of the steps that can be taken to tackle the issue, they may not have a complete understanding of the causes and the wide range of measures available to them to reduce their own impact.

The purpose of this site is to give people a better understanding of what exactly causes the changing of the climate and to inform them of the options that are available to them as an individual to prevent it from getting worse. Alongside the science behind the issue, we will explore current climate modeling techniques, lifestyle changes that can be made, technologies for reducing carbon dioxide in the atmosphere, how money can be invested to tackle the issue, and the steps that can be taken to ensure countries and businesses are playing their part as well. The site is aimed at people with a reasonable understanding of scientific concepts, but more specifically older teenagers who want to reduce their own impact.
